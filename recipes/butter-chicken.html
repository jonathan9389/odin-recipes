Butter Chicken

About the Recipe

Rich, creamy, and full of flavor, Butter Chicken is India’s iconic comfort food. Tender chicken pieces simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy with aromatic spices, perfect with naan or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp of butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn white on all sides.
  4. Stir in the red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the chicken.
  5. Add the tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates from the mixture.
  6. Reduce the heat and pour in the fresh cream. Stir well and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is creamy.
  7. Add the remaining 1 tbsp butter and garam masala. Mix gently.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
