Masala Dosa

About this Recipe

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish consisting of a crispy fermented rice crepe filled with spiced potatoes. It’s light, flavorful, and perfect for breakfast or snacks.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 4-5 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • Fresh curry leaves, a few
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal with fenugreek seeds for 6-8 hours. Grind into a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  2. For filling, heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, and green chilies. Sauté until onions are soft.
  3. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes. Mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes. Adjust salt. Set aside.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan. Pour a ladle of batter and spread thinly in a circular motion to form a dosa.
  5. Drizzle oil around edges. Cook until golden brown.
  6. Place a portion of potato filling in the center and fold dosa over.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
