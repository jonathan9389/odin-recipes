Biryani

About this Recipe

Biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, meat, and sometimes vegetables. Perfect for celebrations or a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or lamb pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a large pan, saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken, turmeric, chili powder, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until chicken is partially done.
  5. Add yogurt, mix well, and cook until meat is tender.
  6. In another pot, boil rice until 70% cooked. Drain water.
  7. Layer rice over meat in a heavy-bottomed pan. Sprinkle coriander leaves.
  8. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes for flavors to blend.
  9. Serve hot with raita or salad.
